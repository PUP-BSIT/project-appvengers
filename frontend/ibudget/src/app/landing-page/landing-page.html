iBudget is a budgeting tool designed to simplify money management. It records income and expenses, categorizes them for better organization, and helps you track savings.

Powerful Features

Smart tools designed to help you take control of your financial future.

AI Bonzi Buddy

Meet your personal AI financial assistant. Bonzi analyzes your real data to provide context-aware advice, remembers your conversations, and can even help you navigate the app via chat.

Smart Notifications

Stay on track with urgency-based alerts. We group updates by time and category (Budgets, Savings, Bills) so you never miss a high-priority financial milestone.

Seamless Tracking

Easily add, edit, and delete transactions. Our clean, intuitive interface makes managing your daily cash flow effortless and organized.

Auto-Categorization

Spend less time sorting. iBudget intelligently categorizes your transactions so you can see exactly where your money is going at a glance.

Goals & Budgets

Turn dreams into reality. Set specific financial goals and create strict budgets with real-time progress tracking to keep you disciplined.

Visual Analytics

Data meets beauty. Interactive charts and graphs powered by Chart.js transform boring numbers into deep, actionable financial insights.

Bank-Level Security

Your privacy is our priority. We use JWT authentication and advanced rate limiting to ensure your financial data remains secure and encrypted.
